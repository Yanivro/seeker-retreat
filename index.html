Everything You Need to Know

Who is this retreat for?

This retreat is for seekers — people who long for deep, meaningful connections but keep finding themselves stuck at surface level. You're tired of small talk. You've tried meetups, therapy, workshops, self-help books......and yet something still blocks depth. If you're ready to learn the mechanics that turn friction into intimacy, you'll feel at home here.

What if I'm introverted or socially anxious?

Perfect. Many participants are introverted, neurodivergent, or socially anxious. We go at the pace of the nervous system. Everything is optional, consent-led, and you have full agency over your participation.

What makes this different from other connection workshops?

Most workshops ask you to “be vulnerable” but skip the hard part: what to do when friction arises. Here, you’ll practice welcoming cancellations, mismatches, and perceived rejections as portals to intimacy—through embodied reps, not just concepts.

What if I don't connect with anyone in the group?

That’s the exact material we work with in real time. If you notice disconnection or doubt, we’ll meet it with tools, curiosity, and facilitation so it can transform—instead of repeating the same pattern.

Do I need prior experience with Authentic Relating or IFS?

No. We welcome beginners and seasoned practitioners alike. You’ll be met exactly where you are and guided step-by-step.

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 60 days before the retreat. 50% refund between 60–30 days. No refunds within 30 days of the retreat start date.

Where do I stay?

Pai offers everything from cozy hostels to boutique resorts. After registration, you’ll receive a curated list of options within walking distance of the venue, plus booking tips and local insights.

How do I get to Pai?

Most fly into Chiang Mai, then travel 3–4 hours to Pai by minibus or private car. We’ll send detailed transportation guidance and recommended providers once you’re registered.

What should I bring?

Comfortable clothing for movement and sitting, a journal, a water bottle, any grounding items you love, and an open heart. A detailed packing list arrives after registration.

What's the group size and demographic?

Maximum 12 participants to protect depth and safety. Ages typically range from late 20s to mid 40s, with diverse identities and backgrounds. All are welcome.
