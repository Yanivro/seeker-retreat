What You'll Actually Learn to Do

Stop Walking on Eggshells

You'll get specific scripts for expressing difficult feelings without your partner shutting down or getting defensive. No more rehearsing conversations in your head for weeks because you're terrified of their reaction.

Stay Calm When They Get Defensive

You'll learn to stay grounded when they start raising their voice, stonewalling, or giving you the silent treatment. Instead of immediately apologizing or taking everything back, you'll work through it together.

Address Issues Before You Explode

You'll catch yourself building resentment early and know exactly how to bring up small issues before they become relationship-ending fights. No more smiling while dying inside until you finally lose it over something tiny.

Be Loved for Who You Actually Are

You'll experience what it feels like to be completely authentic and have someone move closer instead of pulling away. You'll discover that the person you've been hiding is actually more attractive than the 'perfect' version you've been performing.

These aren't theoretical concepts. You'll practice each skill in real-time with feedback and support, so you leave with actual experience, not just knowledge.

Everything You Need to Know

Logistics

How do I secure my spot?

Book a free 15-minute clarity call where we'll discuss your goals, answer questions, and determine if this retreat is right for you. If it's a good fit, we'll send you a secure payment link to reserve your space with a deposit.

What about accommodation in Pai?

We'll provide a detailed list of recommended guesthouses, hostels, and hotels at various price points. Pai has options from budget backpacker places ($10-15/night) to luxury resorts ($100+/night). We'll help you find something that fits your budget and comfort preferences.

How do I get to Pai?

Fly into Chiang Mai (CNX), then take a 3-hour bus ($3) or private transport ($40-60) to Pai. We'll provide detailed travel instructions and can help arrange shared transportation with other participants to split costs.

Safety & Emotional

What if I'm too scared to do this?

That fear is exactly why you need this. We go at a pace your nervous system can handle. You'll never be pushed beyond what feels safe, and you always have permission to step back when needed.

What if I get triggered and can't handle it?

Our entire approach is designed for sensitive nervous systems. We have multiple support systems in place, including one-on-one check-ins and quiet spaces to decompress when needed. We welcome triggers as they are thresholds to deeper connection.

Relationships

What if my partner thinks this is selfish?

Learning to speak your truth actually improves relationships. Many participants find their partners appreciate the more authentic, confident person they become. The skills you learn benefit everyone in your life.

Can I come alone even if I'm in a relationship?

Yes! Many participants come solo. The skills you learn will improve your relationship even if your partner doesn't attend. Some find their partner becomes more interested in growth work after seeing the positive changes.

Inclusion

Do you work with LGBTQ+ and non-traditional relationships?

Absolutely. All relationship styles, orientations, and structures are welcome. People-pleasing affects everyone, and these skills work regardless of who you're in relationship with.

What if I'm single?

These skills work in all relationships - dating, friendships, family, and work. Many single participants use the retreat to break patterns before entering their next relationship.

Practical

What's the refund policy?

Full refund up to 30 days before the retreat. Between 30-14 days: 50% refund. Less than 14 days: no refund but you can transfer to a future retreat.

Is this therapy?

No, this is experiential learning focused on communication skills and relationship patterns. If you're dealing with trauma or mental health concerns, we recommend discussing this work with your therapist first.
